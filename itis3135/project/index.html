Welcome!

Welcome to Charlotte Chain Chasers! We seek to provide a friendly introduction to disc golf. Disc golf is an awesome sport in which people of nearly all ages and skill levels can enjoy. With the sport's explosive growth over the past few years, people from many different walks of life have become interested in it. Whether you are out playing a round with friends, or getting some practice in while solo, disc golf is an easily accessible and deeply enjoyable outdoor activity.
